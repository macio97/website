Real Grass is an addon that allows you to create lawns and gardens with physically accurate grass and weeds models. Based on real world grass shapes, it gives you nice grass distributions among your scene.
The pack comes with:

  • 8 Grass species with 10 variations each
  • 3 Weeds species with 3 variations each
